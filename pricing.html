Data Retention

The data and usage for each course is tracked separately. A course is defined as "active" when students are logging into the course. If an instructor or administrator (enterprise) logs into a course, it does not count as "active". Instructors or administrators (enterprise) can choose manually remove inactive course data before the data is automatically expired.

When data expires or is manually removed, this service does not retain a copy of the data. By only keeping active data in the system, we reduce the overall amount of 'Personally Identifiable Information' (PII) on the system at any time. We feel that this active expiry of data is essential to complying with the spirit and intention of the The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) regulations.
