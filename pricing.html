Pricing

  • Individual - $30 / Month
    This level provides up to 1500 active student seats per year students per course with a data retention policy of one year from active use. Assume 2 semesters per year, 5 classes per semester with 40 students per class, the number of active seats per year for this example would be 2 X 5 X 40 or 400 active seats in a year.

  • Small School - $250 / Month
    This level provides up to 15000 active student seats per year students per course with a data retention policy of one year from active use. Assume 2 semesters per year, 50 classes per semester with 40 students per class, the number of active seats per year for this example would be 2 X 50 X 40 or 4000 active seats in a year.

  • Medium-Sized School - $2000 / Month
    This level provides up to 50000 active student seats per year students per course with a data retention policy of two years from active use.

  • Enterprise - Contact Us
    The Enterprise version of the service is for large schools, schools with particular data retention requirements, or schools with particular data needs. The data for Enterprise customers is kept in a separate database and data can be streamed to customer analytics data systems using Caliper or XAPI protocols. Because Enterprise customers have their own database, it is easy to migrate app stores to/from on-premises hosted application stores. Please contact us at info@learnxp.com for details on the Enterprise version of this service.
